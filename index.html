    Welcome to Windows 98

    The Programming and Game Development Club (ProgDev) is a club that focuses on the programming of useful software that could potentially be used by the school. The club also focuses on game development, allowing students to unleash their creativity and imagination by creating games that could be educational, or just plain fun.

    Project Name - ProgDev Official Home Page

    Project Description:

    The ProgDev Official Home Pade is a webpage for the Programming and Geme Development club. It will showcase the projects and achievements made by the club, and it will also serve as other people's introduction to the club.


    Project Name - SchedScholar

    Project Description:

    SchedScholar is a Discord bot that will send reminders to you to do your tasks/assignments. You can indicate the name of the task to do, as well as the frequency of the reminders.

    Project Name - Overdue

    Project Description:

    Overdue is a Roblox horror game that centers around trying to escape a monster that symbolizes your deadlines. To escape, you must do different tasks around the PSHSSMC campus that correlate to your lessons. The game sheds light on how students procrastinate and how it affects them.

    Side Project Name - Nights at PRINZOP's

    Project Description:

    Nights at PRINZOP's is a Five Nights at Freddy's styled horror game that takes place in a fictional restaurant, with characters such as PRINZOP, Libetario, Ciaming, Poteroti, and Cesco. You must survive the night until 6 AM. There are 6 nights in total, with the final night being the most challenging.


    Side Project Name - Bocchi the Rock Chemistry Quiz

    Project Description:

    Bocchi the Rock Chemistry Quiz is a visual novel that centers around answering chemistry questions that will be asked to you by Hitori Gotou from the famous manga/anime Bocchi the Rock. It's a funny game that can also serve as a basic trivia game for all the chemistry nerds out there.
